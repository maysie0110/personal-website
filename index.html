More About Me

Hi! My name is May Trinh. I am currently an undergraduate Computer Science student from Midwestern State University. I was born in Vietnam, a small country in Southeast Asia, and moved to the U.S. in 2012 looking for a better opportunity. I enjoy learning about technology, especially on how technology was used to improve our everyday lives. Currently, I'm passionate and eager to learn more about technology in the medical field through Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality, as I believe they will grow tremendously in the near future. Aside from that, I'm a self-taught Full-Stack Developer, having worked on several Web Applications.
